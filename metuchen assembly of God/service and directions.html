Directions

METUCHEN ASSEMBLY OF GOD
130 WHITMAN AVE.
METUCHEN, NEW JERSEY 08840


From Rt. 287 North:
Take exit marked Rt. 27 North, Metuchen. Proceed ½ mile and make a right turn onto Rose St. Proceed ½ mile and directly in front of the second stop sign, make a right turn into our driveway.

From Rt. 287 South:
Take New Durham Exit. At light, make left turn onto New Durham. Go to the first signal light and make a right onto Bridge St. Proceed to next traffic light and make a left onto Rt. 27 North. Proceed 4/10 mile and make a right turn onto Rose St. Proceed ½ mile and directly in front of the second stop sign, make a right turn into our driveway.

From Rt. 1 South:
Take exit marked Metuchen & U-Turn. Proceed on Main St. for ½ mile. Turn left onto Brunswick Ave. and go to stop sign. Turn left on Lake Ave., which will become Whitman Ave. in 1/10 mile (at the curve). Proceed another 2/10 mile. At this point Rose St. intersects Whitman Ave. and make an immediate right onto Rose St. and then left up the driveway.

From Rt. 1 North:
Take the exit marked Metuchen, 95, NJ Turnpike. On ramp, follow Metuchen sign. Proceed on Main St. for 4/10 mile and make a left turn onto Brunswick Ave. and go to stop sign. Turn left onto Lake Ave., which will become Whitman Ave. in 1/10 mile (at the curve). Proceed another 2/10 mile. At this point Rose St. intersects Whitman Ave. and make an immediate right onto Rose St. and then left up the driveway.
