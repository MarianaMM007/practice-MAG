NURSERY

The Nursery is a vital ministry in our church. The nursery / toddler staff’s dedication in caring for the children allows parents to fully enjoy the service without worrying about them. We teach children at an early age the love of God. We also create a safe, pleasant, and sanitary environment for infants and toddlers who have been entrusted in our care.
