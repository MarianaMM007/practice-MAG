BIBLE QUIZ MINISTRY

The Bible Quiz Ministry we believe is the greatest tool we can provide our children is the knowledge and love for the Word of God in this time when moral absolutes have been challenged and denounced.

As the Psalmist says in, ‘Psalm 119:11 – I have hidden your Word in my heart that I might not sin against thee’, we hope that our children will be kept safe by the power of God’s Word in their lives.

The children from ages 5 - 17 are learning the Word of God by participating in the Quiz competition organized by the Assembly of God. It is called the Junior Bible Quiz and Teen Bible Quiz. The children are encouraged to study and are taught using a variety of methods that will enable them to live what they learn.
