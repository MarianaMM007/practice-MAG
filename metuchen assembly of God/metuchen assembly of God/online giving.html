GIVE ONLINE

"Honor God with everything you own; give Him the first and the best."
—Proverbs 3:9

Giving begins with the heart. God's heart is generous; He provides us with time, talent, finances and opportunities, asking for only a small portion of these back. As we grow closer to Him, we find ourselves sharing His heart of generosity. We are eager to give to the church, to those with needs in our community and to share His Gospel with the far reaches of the world. Our Online Giving feature provides a simple and secure way to give a gift to Metuchen Assembly of God.
