ROYAL RANGERS

Royal Rangers is a mentoring program for boys (K – High School) that motivate them spiritually through Bible study, Scripture memorization, devotionals, and Christian role models. Boys are strengthened mentally by an advanced awards-based weekly program.

Further, the boys increase their physical prowess by participation in age-specific activities and recreation. Finally, they are introduced to church, community, and nature-related activities to develop their social skills and awareness. We meet on Sundays 4-5:30PM.
