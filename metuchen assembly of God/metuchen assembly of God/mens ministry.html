MEN'S MINISTRY

The purpose of the Men’s Ministry is to help men build an intimate personal relationship with God through Christ and with each other through prayer, Bible study, and fellowship.

The Men’s Fellowship meets once a month on Saturday at 9:30AM for prayer, breakfast, fellowship and topical teaching discussion. The Men’s Fellowship also attends the Honor Bound Men’s Ministries conferences, fishing trips and winter retreats. Men equally meet every second Friday of the month for a time of recreation which further encourages fellowshipping with one another.
